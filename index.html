Coronal Mass Ejections

This is my final paper for the concurrent enrollment course AOSC170: Introduction to Solar System Plasmas. It discusses the basics behind our understanding of Coronal Mass Ejections, how they erupt, evolve through interplanetary space, and have the potential to interact with other magnetized objects. This paper concludes with a discussion of the effects CMEs and other solar eruptions can have on Earth's magnetic field and the repercussions of interaction.
